Cookie Policy

Last updated: December, 2025

1. General Information

This Cookie Policy explains how accessible-ui, uses technologies like cookies and local storage when you visit our website. Our site is designed to be minimalist and does not use tracking or advertising technologies.

2. What are Cookies and Local Storage?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. Local Storage is a similar technology that stores information directly within your browser. Both are used to help websites function correctly.

3. Types of Cookies and Storage We Use

We use only Strictly Necessary cookies and technologies of local storage to ensure the site’s basic functionality and provide a seamless user experience.

Strictly Necessary Cookies:

These cookies are essential for site security and core function. They are used to provide the services you explicitly request (e.g., security features of the hosting platform).

Local Storage (Preference):

This is used to store your consent preference and prevent the cookie notice from appearing on every page view.

4. Third-Party Elements

We rely on third-party service providers for core site elements. We do not control these cookies, and their use is subject to the third party’s privacy policies. These providers may set their own technical cookies:

  • Google Forms: Used for the contact submission form, may set technical cookies required for form security and operation.
  • GitHub Pages: Our hosting service, may set technical cookies necessary for platform stability and security.

5. Your Consent and Management

Since we only use Strictly Necessary cookies and local storage items (including the one that stores your consent preference), prior explicit consent is not required under ePrivacy rules for these specific items.

By continuing to use the site, you acknowledge and agree to the use of these necessary technical elements as described here.

You can always manage or delete cookies via your browser settings. For more details, see your browser’s help section on managing cookies. However, disabling strictly necessary elements may affect the site's functionality.

6. Updates to this Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
