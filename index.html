Why accessibility matters

Accessibility makes digital products usable for all users, regardless of their abilities or devices. This includes people with disabilities, temporary limitations, and users of assistive technologies. Integrating accessibility from the start improves usability, reduces legal and technical risks, and makes interfaces more reliable and intuitive for everyone.
